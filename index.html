Honda City GN Philippines

History

It all started with sir Chan of Chanlimitedlife on Youtube, being one of the very first ones here in the country to have the new GN model Honda city at that time, he could not find any group to find support or to discuss with fellow owners. So he decided to create an FB group, which he initially named "All New 2021 Honda CIty Owners." Other people who saw his videos on Youtube came to fnd the group also and from then on the group grew. around October 2021, the group was renamed "Honda City GN Philippines" to include both the sedan and the newly release hatch variant at that time.
